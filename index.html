One of our standout items on the Healthy Choices menu is the "Grilled Salmon Salad." This vibrant and satisfying salad features a bed of crisp mixed greens, topped with succulent grilled salmon fillet cooked to perfection.

We add a medley of colorful vegetables like cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and red onions for a refreshing crunch. To elevate the flavors, we drizzle a zesty lemon vinaigrette dressing over the salad, adding a tangy twist that perfectly complements the richness of the salmon.

Grilled Salmon Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 salmon fillet
  • 4 cups mixed greens
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season the salmon fillet with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a grill pan over medium heat and brush with olive oil.
  3. Grill the salmon for 4-5 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
  5. Whisk together the olive oil and lemon juice to make the dressing.
  6. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  7. Top the salad with the grilled salmon fillet.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
