Divine Chocolate Trifle Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 chocolate cake, prepared and cooled
  • 2 cups chocolate mousse
  • 1 cup chocolate ganache
  • 1 cup heavy cream, whipped
  • Chocolate shavings for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cut the chocolate cake into small cubes.
  2. In serving glasses or a trifle dish, layer the chocolate cake cubes at the bottom.
  3. Add a layer of chocolate mousse on top of the cake cubes.
  4. Drizzle some chocolate ganache over the mousse layer.
  5. Repeat the layers until the glasses are almost full.
  6. Finish with a final layer of whipped cream on top.
  7. Garnish with chocolate shavings.
  8. Chill in the refrigerator for a few hours before serving.
